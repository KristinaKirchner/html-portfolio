The Best Movies According to Kristina

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Elvis (2022)

This movie was directed by Baz Luhrmann. Austin Butler stars as Elvis Presley in this jaw dropping musical biopic of Elvis's life.

Notorious (1940)

This film was directed by Alfred Hitchcock and directed by David O Selznick. This is a captivating spy film that takes place during Hitler's reign. The characters are exciting and the plot twists are fantastic.

Gone With the Wind (1939)

Such an amazing storyline and classic characters! Many lines from this movie are very famous. The costumes and sets are fantastic and larger than life! The actors are classic Hollywood. This movie has stood the test of time and will forever be timeless.
